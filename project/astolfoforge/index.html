Astolfo Forge Mod

UPDATE: The mod is now available on Modrinth for download! If you prefer that site for downloading, click here.

What does this mod add?

Mobs

  • Astolfo in 3 outfits - Astolfo Schoolgirl, Astolfo Armour, and Astolfo in Casual outfit
  • Felix Argyle
  • Hideri Kanzaki
  • Venti
  • Rimuru
  • Nagisa Shiota
  • Sieg (Fate/Apocrypha)
  • Bridget _(I know she's a transgender girl but I included her because why not)_

Commands

  • /duplicatecuties <amount> <range> - Duplicate all of the added mobs within the range amount times. The mod doesn't have any limits in the 2 parameters, so entering big numbers might cause your game to crash!
  • /despawn <selector> - This command is simple and allows you to "kill" monsters without the animation. This prevents the particles from death spawning and lagging your game if you have too many mobs from this mod spawned.
  • /cuterain <iterations> <range> <height> - This command makes the mobs above rain from the sky. It runs how many times iterations is, spawning mobs in the range provided, spawning them in the specified height. Every iteration spawns 1 of each mob above, excluding creative mobs.

Creative items and mobs

  • Astolfo Attractor - The text "gay" in the menu, attracts only variants of Astolfo.
  • Astolfo Repellent - A Felix Argyle entity which repels all Astolfos from its location.
  • Monster White - No crafting recipe yet, gives you speed and jump boost and gives you trans rights 🏳️‍⚧️❤️

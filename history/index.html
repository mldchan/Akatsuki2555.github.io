History of Akatsuki2555

Also take a look at the website history.

"I", "my" and "me" refers to Akatsuki2555 on this page. I use any pronouns as of now.

Celeste Content

  • I have started doing Celeste content and now YouTube is nagging me to make more of it.
  • This will reduce MSC content, but increase subscribers, views and give me a new community of new poeple.

Participating in MSC challenges

  • Due to lack of content ideas from me, I've started joining MSC challenges hosted on a Discord server.
  • I've so far participated in 5 challenges and won them. -2024/07/26

Slowly quitting My Summer Car modding

  • I am slowly quitting MSC modding mainly because I run other more successfully projects.
  • I am probably still going to update my mods to work properly, but seeing new mods will lower.
  • I am still going to provide help to some extent to people.

Adding variety of content - July 2024

  • I started experimenting with new content.
  • So far I am noticing that Celeste content is getting popular on my channel and a beta experiment from YouTube recommended me to upload more Celeste related videos.

Moving to Nekoweb

  • After I created my Codeberg account, I also moved my website from GitHub to Codeberg. When I realized how unstable Codeberg Pages was (over 14 hours of downtime at once!), I moved my site to Nekoweb.

Pronoun Change - December 2023

  • In December 2023, I decided to change my pronouns to she/her, receiving support from most of the servers I was part of.

Starting Learning Japanese - November 2023

  • In November 2023, I started learning Japanese, using Duolingo and other resources such as dictionaries to improve my language skills.

Popular Mod and TikTok

  • In 2023, I released the popular mod "Asphalt Roads" for My Summer Car, which gained significant attention on NexusMods.
  • I started my TikTok account in August 2023, gaining popularity and followers, especially after a false ban from a Discord server for alleged NSFW content.

Refreshed Modding Tutorials

  • I refreshed my My Summer Car modding tutorials, uploading a comprehensive tutorial that replaced my older ones.

SEAJAM and Mon Bazou

  • In 2022, I discovered Mon Bazou and created tutorials, guides, and gameplay videos on editing save files and other aspects of the game.

Raspberry Pi Server

  • I acquired a Raspberry Pi and started using it as a server for hosting my projects, including Akabot.

First My Summer Car Mod - December 31st, 2020

  • On December 31st, 2020, I uploaded my first My Summer Car mod, "OldCarSounds," which recreated the old car sounds from previous alpha versions of the game.

My Summer Car Modding Tutorials

  • I uploaded a series of My Summer Car modding tutorials, covering topics such as setting up the development environment, accessing playmaker variables, creating GUI, saving and loading data, and making objects pickupable.
  • I also uploaded a few Among Us videos during this time but stopped due to the game's declining popularity.

Further Expansion

  • I built the Satsuma in a My Summer Car playthrough without cheats, although I didn't successfully complete the game.
  • I added Minecraft plugins and virtual machine content to my channel.

Internet Upgrade

  • I upgraded my internet speed from 10mbps to 100mbps, enabling me to upload videos at a faster rate.

Expansion of Content - December 2019

  • In December 2019, I started uploading videos of other games, such as Rust, which coincided with a server hack during the Christmas event.
  • I expanded my content to include programming tutorials, covering topics like discord.js and My Summer Car mods.
  • I also documented and uploaded videos of power outages in my area.

Early Content

  • I focused on uploading videos of the game "My Summer Car," including unique content like filling the entire highway with beer cases.

Introduction - November 26th, 2019

  • I (previously known as mldkyt) created my YouTube channel on November 26th, 2019.
  • Initially, I uploaded my previously edited content, which took a few days due to my slow internet connection.

This summarizes the history of my YouTube channel, highlighting my content, achievements, and personal journey.
